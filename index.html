About Us

At IMPACT Youth, we empower Muslim students to give back to their communities. We started as a student-led club, partnered with our school and local masjid, and grew into a registered non-profit.

Team

  • Taha Rastegar, Founder — Started it all.
  • Mohammad Kiaei, Lead Software Coordinator — Uses coding and math to serve the community.
  • John Zaidi, Lead UI Designer — Programmer interested in biotechnology.
  • Mohammad Amin Shahrabi, Social Media Manager — Devoted to community service.

Our Impact

We’ve led food and clothes drives, built care packages, hosted at the SABA Eid festival, and offered free car washes.
